Software Development

Employee Wellness Platform

Using Rails, VUE, and PostgreSQL on Heroku and Vercel cloud infrastructure  iota  developed a scalable app platform for a client serving large institutional partners. The app provided a desktop interface for administrators managing team wellness requirements (for example, university vaccination records), alongside mobile app access for team members. API integration with PeopleSoft records. Automated reporting and compliance requirements.

Security

Detect Fraudulent Traffic

 iota  engineers implemented live-wire machine learning techniques to monitor 50k+ requests per minute to detect and block fraudulent ad traffic. Impacted the bottom line by increasing revenue and preventing unauthorized access to user transactions.

Data Analysis

Social Media Segmentation

During the COVID-19 Public Health Emergency,  iota  team worked with partners at the Center for Disease Control to collect and study millions of vaccine-hesitant messages online with the goal of tailoring a nation-wide social media campaign to target at at-risk demographics, measurably improving public health outcomes.
